About

T.T. Gibran is an alumnus of the Clarion West and Tin House workshops. His narrative frameworks apply structural rigour to the ancestral memory of his Black (South Carolina) and Palestinian (Haifa) lineage.

His work has been recognized by elite institutions across the speculative fiction landscape. He lives in Los Angeles.
